Foreign Merchants of Socotra
  • Foreign Merchants of Socotra

During the early first millennium CE the island of Socotra, off the coasts of Yemen and Somalia, was a hubb of traders from all corners of the Indian Ocean. This website aims to gather all the primary sources on the island in a centralized digital space.
